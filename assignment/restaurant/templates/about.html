About Us

Step into the heart of Singapore’s rich culinary heritage at Yiyi's, where tradition meets taste.

At Yiyi's, we pride ourselves on serving authentic local delicacies and handcrafted beverages that capture the spirit of Singapore’s rich culture.

Whether you’re here to enjoy a hearty breakfast, a mid-day snack, or a leisurely coffee break, our menu offers something for everyone.

From the aroma of freshly brewed kopi to the irresistible crunch of kaya toast, every bite and sip takes you on a journey of nostalgia and comfort.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space where friends and families gather to savor cherished traditions.

Come and experience the essence of Singapore’s cafe culture, where every visit is more than a meal— it’s a moment to relish. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
